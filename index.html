Billionaires Across the Globe

The visualisation shows the count of billionaires in each country. The U.S. still has more billionaires than any other country, with 724 worth (up from 614 last year). China is closing the gap with 626 (excluding 71 from Hong Kong and 1 from Macao). Last year the U.S. had 614 billionaires and China had 456 (including 66 from Hong Kong and 1 Macao). As a result of the gains in China, Beijing is home to more billionaires than anywhere in the world, overtaking New York City. India has the third highest number of billionaires, with 140. Altogether, the 1,149 billionaires from Asia-Pacific countries are worth $4.7 trillion, while the U.S. billionaires are worth a total $4.4 trillion.
